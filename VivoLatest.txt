================================================================================
VIVO X200 ULTRA - PREMIUM FLAGSHIP SPECIFICATIONS & FEATURES
================================================================================

The Vivo X200 Ultra is Vivo's top-tier, camera-centric flagship smartphone, 
unveiled as the photography powerhouse of the X200 series. It combines the
cutting-edge Snapdragon 8 Elite platform with a class-leading ZEISS imaging system.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PERFORMANCE & HARDWARE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite (built on an advanced 3nm process).
* GPU: Adreno 830, offering next-generation mobile graphics, advanced gaming 
  performance, and hardware-accelerated ray tracing.
* Dedicated Imaging Chip: Proprietary custom V-series imaging chip (V4) designed 
  by Vivo to offload complex computational photography and video algorithms.
* Memory: Configurations of 12GB or 16GB high-speed LPDDR5X RAM.
* Storage: 256GB, 512GB, or 1TB of ultra-fast UFS 4.0 storage.
* Cooling: Vapor chamber liquid cooling system to maintain sustained peak performance.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. PROFESSIONAL ZEISS CAMERA SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Rear Camera Array:
  - 50MP Main Camera: Large 1-inch (or equivalent advanced custom) sensor, 
    f/1.75 aperture, Optical Image Stabilization (OIS), and ZEISS T* Coating
    to significantly reduce lens flare and ghosting.
  - 200MP Periscope Telephoto: 1/1.4" large sensor, f/2.67 aperture, OIS, 
    ZEISS APO certification (apochromatic lens design for superior color correction), 
    3.7x optical zoom, and up to 100x digital zoom. Supports macro mode down to 10cm.
  - 50MP Ultrawide Camera: f/2.0 aperture, 119-degree field of view, low-distortion 
    optics, and autofocus for wide-angle macros.
* Front Camera:
  - 50MP Selfie Camera: f/2.0 aperture, autofocus support, and advanced beauty 
    and portrait algorithms.
* Video Recording: Up to 8K resolution video, 4K HDR at 120fps, and specialized 
  ZEISS Cinematic Video portrait modes.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. DISPLAY & DESIGN
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Screen: 6.82-inch LTPO AMOLED curved display.
* Resolution: QHD+ (1440 x 3168 pixels) with sharp 518 ppi density.
* Refresh Rate: Dynamic 1Hz to 120Hz refresh rate (LTPO technology) for optimized
  power efficiency.
* Brightness: Up to 3,000+ nits peak HDR brightness for excellent outdoor visibility.
* Eye Care: High-frequency 2160Hz PWM dimming to reduce eye strain in low-light environments.
* Build Quality: Premium glass-sandwich design protected by "Armor Glass" on front 
  and back, with an elegant circular metallic ring housing the camera array.
* Durability: IP68 and IP69 rating for advanced dust and water resistance.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. BATTERY & CHARGING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Battery Capacity: Massive 6,000 mAh silicon-carbon/semi-solid-state battery,
  providing exceptional density and longevity in cold weather.
* Wired Charging: 90W Ultra-Fast FlashCharge.
* Wireless Charging: 40W Wireless FlashCharge.
* Reverse Charging: Supports reverse wired and wireless charging to power accessories.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. CONNECTIVITY, SECURITY & SOFTWARE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Operating System: OriginOS 5 (based on Android 15), featuring Vivo's BlueLM AI
  suite for writing assistance, smart transcription, and real-time voice translation.
* Security: Ultra-fast 3D Ultrasonic under-display fingerprint sensor and AI 
  Face Unlock.
* Networks: Dual-SIM 5G, Wi-Fi 7 (802.11be), Bluetooth 5.4, and Dual-frequency GPS.
* Extra Features: Infrared remote control blaster, NFC, and high-performance stereo speakers.
